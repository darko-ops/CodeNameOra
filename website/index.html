The line we don't cross

Your music stays yours

Dromo analyzes tempo right on your iPhone, against music that's already there. The only thing that ever leaves your device is a number — a song's beats-per-minute — never a single second of audio.

That discipline builds something useful: a shared map of tempo facts that makes the next run start instantly, without re-analyzing the same song twice. Facts about music, not copies of it.

  • On-device analysisTempo is computed locally, never in the cloud.
  • Numbers onlyBPM and energy leave the phone — audio never does.
  • No account required to runConnect a service when you want; skip it when you don't.
